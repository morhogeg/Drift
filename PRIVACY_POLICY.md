# Privacy Policy — Drift

_Last updated: June 10, 2026_

Drift ("the app") is built to keep your thinking private. This policy explains
what data the app handles and how. In short: your conversations live on your
device, you use your own AI key, and we don't track you or sell anything.

## Who provides the app
Drift is developed by Mor Hogeg. For any privacy question, contact:
**morhogeg@gmail.com**

## Your AI key and your prompts (bring-your-own-key)
Drift does not provide a shared AI backend. You supply your own Google Gemini
API key, which is stored locally on your device. When you send a message, your
prompt and conversation context are sent **directly from your device to Google**
under your own key, subject to Google's API and privacy terms. The developer
does not operate a server in between and does not receive, log, or store your
prompts or the AI's responses.

## Data stored on your device
Your chats, drifts, snippets, settings, and your API key are stored locally on
your device (in the app's local storage). They remain on your device unless you
choose to enable backup (below).

## Optional cloud backup (Apple Sign-In)
Backup and restore is **opt-in** and off by default. If you choose to sign in
with Apple and enable backup:

- **Account data:** We receive your name and email address from Apple solely to
  identify your account for backup. (If you use Apple's "Hide My Email," we only
  ever see the relay address.)
- **Your content:** A copy of your chats and drifts is stored in our cloud
  backend (Google Firebase) so you can restore them on your devices.
- **API keys are never backed up.** Before any backup leaves your device, your
  API keys are stripped out. They are never transmitted to or stored in the
  cloud.

You can stop backing up and delete your backed-up data at any time by signing
out and requesting deletion at the contact email above.

## What we do NOT do
- We do **not** use third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
- We do **not** track you across apps or websites.
- We do **not** sell or share your personal data.

## Third-party services
- **Google Gemini API** — receives your prompts under your own key. See Google's
  privacy terms.
- **Google Firebase** — used only if you enable cloud backup, to authenticate
  your account and store your backups.
- **Apple Sign-In** — used only if you choose it, to authenticate your account.

## Children
Drift is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data
from them.
