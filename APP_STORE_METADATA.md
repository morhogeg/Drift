## Description  (max 4000)
```
Drift is an AI chat built for thinking, not just answering.

Most AI chats trap every idea in one long, scrolling thread. Drift lets you follow your curiosity sideways: highlight any word or phrase in a reply, and branch it into a focused side-conversation — a "drift" — without losing your place. Explore the tangent, then push the insight back into the main chat or save it for later.

As you explore, Drift quietly builds a living map of everything you've branched into — a knowledge tree you can pan and zoom to see how your ideas connect.

WHY DRIFT IS DIFFERENT

• Branch any idea — Highlight text, drift into it, explore as deep as you want. Your main conversation stays intact.
• Lenses — One tap to Simplify a hard idea, take a Deep Dive, see what a concept Connects to, or Challenge a claim.
• Knowledge tree — A spatial map of every branch you've explored, so your thinking has a shape you can navigate.
• Built for depth — Clean, dark, distraction-free. Designed to feel like the smartest place to think.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN

• Bring your own key — Drift uses your own Google Gemini API key (free to get). Your prompts go straight to Google under your key. We're not in the middle, and there's no subscription.
• Your data is yours — Conversations are stored on your device. Optional iCloud-style backup with Apple Sign-In is fully under your control, and your API keys are never included in any backup.
• No ads, no tracking — Drift doesn't track you or sell anything.

Drift is for the curious — students, researchers, writers, and anyone who thinks best by following one question into the next.

Download Drift and give your ideas room to branch.
```
